# Changelog Galantis Kernel

1. Disabled GENTLE_FAIR_SLEEPERS.
2. Reduced swappiness from 60 to 40.
3. Optimized SLUB.
4. Asynchronous I/O latency to a solid-state disk greatly increased.
5. NOATIME and NODIRATIME by default.
6. Enabled aggressive multi-core power savings.
7. Dynamic readahead support.
8. Add Sound Control 3.4.
9. Added governors (-Ondemandplus -lionheart -wheatley -intelliactive -intellidemand).
